About Nikki

About Nikki

Nikki is a Highly Specialist Speech and Language Therapist with 20 years experience, providing therapy and assessment to children in NHS, Education and as an Independent practitioner.

Nikki is also a qualified Sensory Integration Practitioner.

Lots of children with additional needs don't have access to the therapy they need to reach their full potential. Growing Hope Farnham runs in collaboration with Jubilee Church, Farnham, with a vision to see all children and families thrive.

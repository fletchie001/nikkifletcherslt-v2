Privacy Policy

This privacy policy sets out how Nikki Fletcher SLT collects, uses and protects any information you provide.

What information is collected

When you contact Nikki Fletcher SLT, you may be asked to provide your name, contact information, and details about your child's needs. This information is used solely to provide the services requested.

How information is used

Information collected is used to provide speech and language therapy services, to communicate with you about your child's care, and to comply with legal and professional obligations including HCPC registration requirements.

Data security

All client information is stored securely and confidentially in accordance with GDPR regulations and RCSLT/HCPC guidelines. Information will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except where required by law or professional duty of care obligations.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact: nikkifslt@gmail.com
